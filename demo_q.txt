What is another name for a car?
Automobile or motor vehicle.

Who built the first true automobile?
Nicolas-Joseph Cugnot.

What powered Cugnot’s first automobile?
Steam.

What type of engine became the standard for cars?
Gasoline engine.

Who built the first successful gasoline-powered car in the United States?
Charles and J. Frank Duryea.

Who invented the assembly line for making cars?
Henry Ford.

What was the name of Ford’s popular car model?
Model T.

How did cars change where people could live?
People could live farther from their jobs.

What new services developed because of cars?
Gas stations and convenience stores.

What are some ways cars have changed over time?
They have new technologies, safety features, and alternative fuels.
